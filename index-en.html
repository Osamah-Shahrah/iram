About Us

IRAM International for Contracting and Logistics Services was established in 2015 to meet the market's needs for contracting and logistics services with modern techniques that make us stand out from others. This is achieved by adopting a qualified and experienced staff, believing that every individual in the IRAM team is a partner in profit and success. This makes everyone in IRAM's team feel satisfied and reassured, with no other option but to succeed and achieve complete development to provide an exemplary service to the customer in all administrative, financial, and technical aspects, believing that true success is customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

IRAM International strives to be one of the best service and commercial groups worldwide in the field of contracting and logistics by implementing strategic plans in all service and commercial fields it provides. IRAM International also seeks to have branches all over the world to provide all its distinguished services.

Our Mission

To provide a range of integrated services to clients, according to the highest international standards that ensure customer satisfaction and work quality.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of integrated logistics services to ensure smooth operations

Land Transportation Services

Safe and efficient transportation of goods across a wide network of local and regional roads, with a modern and diverse fleet that meets all sizes and weights.

Warehousing and Inventory Management

Modern warehouses equipped with the latest technologies to ensure the safety of goods, with advanced systems for efficient inventory management and distribution.

Agricultural Logistics Services

Specialized logistics solutions for the agricultural sector and fresh produce, including refrigerated transport and storage in controlled environments to maintain quality.

Express Air Freight

Quality Management and Excellence

Quality Management

We consider quality management a fundamental belief, as it leads to success through joint and continuous efforts in planning, developing, and improving work performance and services provided to customers continuously to achieve complete and continuous customer satisfaction.

Time Management

We consider planned time in work time management as a strategy and conscious control of time spent on specific activities to increase performance and efficiency or outputs that suit the client's device. IRAM also provides useful plans through a set of skills, tools, and techniques used to manage time and achieve projects and specific goals compatible with the entrusted work date. Time management is achieved through a planned set of operations, tools, techniques, and methods. Time management in any project is necessary to improve and achieve goals.

Risk Management

Specialized management measures and evaluates risks, then develops a strategy for their management. IRAM International relies on specific strategies to transfer risks to other parties, avoid them, reduce their negative impacts, lower them to acceptable levels, and mitigate potential risks.

Human Resources Management

This refers to how IRAM International manages employee skills and working cadres, focusing on attracting and retaining effective professional competencies by analyzing job realities and requirements, and through successful planning to secure a distinguished and suitable cadre for work needs.

Communication Management

The concept of communication in business and project management aims to select appropriate methods for communication between departments and employees, and between departments and clients. It is essential to identify key information that needs to be conveyed to all parties. IRAM International aims to improve the level of interaction between the work team and clients to enhance overall work management. Communication management also helps in documenting information for both parties, facilitating information exchange between different departments, and monitoring work progress.

Development Management

We focus on developing the skills of employees and working cadres by analyzing the reality and requirements of work and successful planning to secure a distinguished cadre suitable for work needs, ensuring continuous development and innovation in our services.
